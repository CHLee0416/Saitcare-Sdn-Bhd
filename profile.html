About Us

SAITCARE HUB SDN. BHD.

SaitCare Hub Sdn. Bhd. Company is a company that provides printing and repairing electronics services for the students of UTHM and people around Parit Raja，Johor.Till this day,the SaithCare Hub Sdn. Vision and Mission

Mission

To deliver unprecedented and simple solutions deemed necessary not luxury for business without compromising quality and price. In the mission statement of SaitCare Hub Sdn Bhd, we can see that the owner of this company is very much thinking of a simple solution and will not compromise on quality and price. It is a great mission statement because it describes how it goes about its business.

Vision

To include the element of relationships in business to enhance customer satisfaction this leads to loyalty and retention. In the vision statement of SaitCare Hub Sdn Bhd, it makes it clear that it is all about its customers. SaitCare Hub Sdn Bhd's aim is to only offer its customers the best which is the best products, the best services and the best experience possible. Its vision statement perfectly describes its company spirit of going above and beyond.
